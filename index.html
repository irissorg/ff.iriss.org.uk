Welcome! We’re glad you could make it

Fit for the Future is a project dedicated to inspiring better outcomes for older people in Scotland by working with providers from the independent sector. This work is a partnership between Iriss and Scottish Care and is focused on supporting the independent sector to engage with the challenges and opportunities of the future. From our close working with providers, older people and their carers we have developed a series of case studies which we hope will inspire and inform our audience to embrace change.

The resources are split into four categories...

  • Small steps

    Little things to try today

  • Walk

    Testing new things

  • Run

    Transformative change

  • Fly

    The future of care

The former categories are about small changes that you could make today, and the latter are longer, more challenging projects. We recommend that you start where you’re comfortable or if you’re not sure... start from the start!
